Web accessibility is important.

This website provides at-a-glance accessibility guidelines so you can stay in your flow. No more scrolling through wordy PDFs or outdated websites. We have categorized and simplified WCAG 2.0 so it is quick to browse and easy to understand.

Information & References

Please note that the content of this website is a condensed version of the WCAG 2.0 standards, and some specific information may be missing because it's not significantly relevant to the webdev workflow.

The standards discussed were taken from WebAIM's WCAG checklist and W3's WCAG 2.1 reference.
