Animation

The accessibility of page animations are often overlooked. Follow these guidelines to ensure that your site animations are properly set up.


Flashing AnimationsLevel A

A graphic representing flashing content.

Do not include any animations on your site that flash more than 3 times per second. This can be ignored if the flashing content is small and the flashes are lower contrast/do not contain a significant amount of red.

Enhanced FlashingLevel AAA

No content on your page flashes more than 3 times a second, including small elements.


Animated InteractionLevel AAA

A graphic representing user triggered animations.

Include a mechanism for users to disable animations and movements triggered by user interaction that are not essential.
